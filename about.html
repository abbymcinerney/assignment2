A Bit About Me

My name is Abby (short for Abigail), I'm 19 years old and live in Hertfordshire.
I'm currently an undergraduate student in my first year at the University of Northampton, where I study Business Computing (Web Design).
I chose to study this after taking an interest in computing while taking my GCSE's. I find computing, coding in particular, very rewarding which is what motivates me within the subject. Throughout school art was never a strong point of mine so I found that programming/web development was a better way for me to get creative.
I hope to graduate University, not only with high grades but also with as much

industry experience as possible. During my first year of Sixth Form I visited a Computercenter office in Budapest to shadow some of their employees in different departments.
This was the first time I saw the way computing was applied within a large company and allowed me to work out which aspects of computing I was most interested in as well as the aspects that were not as suited to me. This was an extremely valuable experience to me and I hope to complete more work shadowing/experiences in the future.
